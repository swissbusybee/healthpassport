About Us

Health Passport was developed to solve a real world problem of not having vaccination & critical health data in a central location.

We want to provide a digital solutuon that helps people stay organized with their vaccinations and critical health information as well as manage records for their families.

We hope that this solution helps people in all locations and from all walks of life keep themselves and their families safe and healthy.
